How to get involved:

  1. Assess the needs on the ground for 1-3 areas your sangha can support.
  2. Identify volunteer opportunities, supplies needed and city/county-approved organizations to potentially partner with.
  3. Identify key disaster-relief decision makers in the city.
  4. Identify reliable online resources for information.
  5. Mobilize a committed volunteer team who can serve on the ground (minimum one week, to begin with)
