Hurricane Helene Relief Efforts


How to get involved
  1. Assess the needs on the ground for 1-3 areas your sangha can support.
  2. Identify volunteer opportunities, supplies needed and city/county-approved organizations to potentially partner with.
  3. Identify key disaster-relief decision makers in the city.
  4. Identify reliable online resources for information.
  5. Mobilize a committed volunteer team who can serve on the ground (minimum one week, to begin with)

Fill provide team's information, county and city adopted, and relief plan at this shared google sheet.

We have created a Whatsapp Group to providate updates on the list of supplies needed at different sites Link to join the "Supplies & Donations" Whatsapp Group.

Please shared pictures of your team's relief efforts, along with captions, with Siggy Meir at smeier@artofliving.org or upload to Dropbox here.
